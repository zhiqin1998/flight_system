Bomb threat cancels classes at South LA’s Locke High School; prom will go on

Authorities are investigating a bomb threat Friday, May 3, 2019, involving Locke High School in South Los Angeles. (Google Street View)
By CITY NEWS SERVICE |
PUBLISHED: May 3, 2019 at 10:38 am | UPDATED: May 3, 2019 at 11:48 am
LOS ANGELES — Authorities were searching the campus of Locke High School on Friday in the South Los Angeles area after a bomb threat was received, but no explosives were found.

The threat was received about 7 a.m. and prompted authorities to secure the campus at San Pedro and 111th streets, according to the Los Angeles Police Department.

A bomb squad searched the campus for about three hours, and no explosives were found, police said.

Classes were canceled for the day, but the school’s prom was still set for Friday night, according to KNX1070.
